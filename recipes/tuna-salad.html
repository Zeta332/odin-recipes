Bananabread

Description

A quick and refreshing tuna salad made with canned tuna, crisp vegetables, and a creamy dressing. Perfect for sandwiches, wraps, or a light meal, this easy recipe is healthy, flavorful, and ready in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna
  • 2-3 tbsp mayo
  • 1 celary stalk
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Drain the tuna and put it in a bowl.
  2. MAdd mayo and chopped celery.
  3. Mix well and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve on bread, crackers, or salad.
