Bananabread

Description

A soft, moist banana bread made with ripe bananas, a touch of sweetness, and a warm hint of vanilla. This easy recipe comes together in minutes and bakes into a golden, flavorful loaf that’s perfect for breakfast, snacks, or dessert. No mixer needed — just mash, mix, bake, and enjoy a deliciously comforting homemade treat.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. Mash the bananas in a bowl.
  3. Mix in the melted butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir until combined.
  5. Pour batter into a greased loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let cool slightly, slice, and enjoy!
