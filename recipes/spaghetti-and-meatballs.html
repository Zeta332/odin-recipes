Spaghetti and meatballs

Description

Spaghetti with meatballs is a classic comfort dish made of tender ground-meat balls simmered in a rich tomato sauce and served over hot spaghetti. The flavor comes from a mix of herbs, garlic, and Parmesan in the meatballs, plus a slowly cooked sauce that ties everything together.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • egg
  • breadcrumbs
  • garlic
  • crushed tomatoes
  • spaghetti
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef, egg, breadcrumbs, salt, pepper, and a little garlic.
  2. Roll the mixture into small meatballs.
  3. Fry the meatballs in a bit of oil until browned.
  4. Pour the crushed tomatoes into the same pan. Add salt/pepper.
  5. Simmer the meatballs in the sauce for about 20 minutes.
  6. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until done.
  7. Drain the pasta and serve with meatballs and sauce on top.
